About Heidi Carter

Heidi Carter is a North Carolina native and has lived in Durham with her husband Scott for 40 years. Heidi graduated from Duke University in 1983 with a BS in Biology and went on to receive a Masters of Science in Medicinal Chemistry from UNC in 1984 and a Masters of Public Health from UNC Gillings School of Global Public Health in 2010. She has 4 grown children, all of whom are graduates of Durham Public Schools and are now working as a doctor, lawyer, physical therapist, and automotive technician. Heidi is an avid runner and loves to cook, read, and bike. She also enjoys spending time with her first grandson, Lou.

For the past fifteen years, twelve on the Board of Education and three as a County Commissioner, it has been one of my high honor to serve all of Durham County, and I remain committed to this diverse, complex and dynamic community we love so much. Our County has given much to my family and me, and I want to give back by helping everyone enjoy the wonderful quality of life Durham offers, especially those who are most vulnerable and have been by-passed by Durhamâ€™s prosperity. During my first term as Commissioner, we made numerous accomplishments and began many promising new initiatives. I cherish the chance to serve again to steer them through to completion.
